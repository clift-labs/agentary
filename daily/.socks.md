# Daily Logs

Daily notes and reflections.

## Format

Each day gets a file: `YYYY-MM-DD.md`

```markdown
---
title: "Daily Log - YYYY-MM-DD"
created: YYYY-MM-DD
tags: [daily]
---

## Today's Focus

Main priorities for the day.

## Notes

Things that came up during the day.

## Reflection

End of day thoughts.
```
